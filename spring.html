Spring Track

What You'll Do in Spring Track

The Cross Country course teaches students the fundamentals of distance running, stamina building, and race strategies. Students will participate in both individual and team events, competing in various cross country races, while developing skills in pacing, endurance, and mental toughness.

Events

  • Sprints: Includes 100m, 200m, and 400m dash events testing top speed and acceleration.
  • Middle Distance: 800m and 1500m races focusing on aerobic capacity and race strategy.
  • Distance: Includes 1600m, 1 mile, 3200m, and 2 mile runs for stamina and pacing precision.
  • Hurdles: 100m/110m high hurdles and 400m intermediate hurdles emphasizing rhythm and agility.
  • Relays: 4x100, 4x200, 4x400, 4x800, sprint medley, and distance medley relays showcase team speed and chemistry.
  • Throws: Shot put, discus, javelin, and hammer throw challenge strength and technique.
  • Jumps: Long jump, triple jump, high jump, and pole vault requiring explosive power and control.
