Why you should do track:

  • Improves Fitness – Enhances speed, endurance, strength, and overall athleticism.
  • Team & Individual Growth – Compete both individually and as part of a supportive team.
  • Fun & Social – Meet new people, make friends, and be part of a motivated community.
  • Great for Cross-Training – Helps athletes in other sports improve their speed and endurance.
