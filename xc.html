Cross Country

What You'll Do in Cross Country

Cross country is a great way for athletes to build endurance, improve their physical fitness, and develop mental toughness. It teaches discipline, perseverance, and goal-setting, all while creating a strong sense of teamwork and community. Plus, running outdoors through parks and trails makes it a fun and rewarding experience!

Cross Country Events

  • 5K Races: The standard varsity distance raced across most invitationals and championship meets in NJ.
  • 2 Mile Races: Typically run by freshmen or JV runners to build experience and endurance.
  • Course Variety: Races take place on trails, grass, and hills — often in parks like Holmdel, Thompson, and Ocean County.
  • Team Scoring: Cross country emphasizes team performance, with the top five runners' placements determining team scores.
  • Championship Levels: Includes dual meets, conference, county, sectional, group, and Meet of Champions.
