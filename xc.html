Cross Country

What You'll Do in Cross Country

The Cross Country course teaches students the fundamentals of distance running, stamina building, and race strategies. Students will participate in both individual and team events, competing in various cross country races, while developing skills in pacing, endurance, and mental toughness.

Events

  • Monmouth Battlefield: A challenging 5K race through scenic terrain.
  • Ocean County Park: A fast-paced race featuring a variety of terrain.
  • Holmdel Park: Known for its hilly course, perfect for testing endurance.
  • Thompson Park - 5K: A competitive event that focuses on stamina and pacing.
  • Garrett Mountain: A challenging 5K with varying elevations.
  • Warinanco Park: A fun yet demanding 5K race perfect for building stamina.

Big Brook Park is a scenic location with great trails for training and races.

Holmdel Park is known for its challenging hills and historic race events.
