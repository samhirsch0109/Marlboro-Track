Winter Track

Why You Should Join Winter Track:

Winter track is a great way for athletes to stay in shape, build speed and endurance, and prepare for spring sports. It teaches discipline, goal-setting, and mental toughness, all in a supportive team environment. Plus, competing indoors adds a fun and unique twist to track season!

Winter Track Events

  • Sprints: Includes 55m, 100m, 200m, and 400m dash events testing explosive speed and power.
  • Middle Distance: 600m, 800m, 1000m, and 1500m races focusing on endurance and pacing.
  • Distance: Events such as the 1600m, mile, 3000m, and 3200m testing stamina and strategy.
  • Hurdles: High, low, and intermediate hurdles including 55m hurdles and 400m hurdles.
  • Relays: Includes 4x100, 4x200, 4x400, 4x800, sprint medley, distance medley, shuttle hurdles, and mixed 4x400 relays.
  • Throws: Shot put, discus, hammer, and javelin events testing strength and technique.
  • Jumps: High jump, long jump, triple jump, and pole vault requiring speed, agility, and coordination.

Ocean Breeze Athletic Complex in Staten Island hosts some of the biggest and most competitive indoor track meets in the region, featuring a fast track and top-tier facilities.

Many winter track meets are held at the Bubble, offering athletes a consistent and competitive indoor venue throughout the season.
